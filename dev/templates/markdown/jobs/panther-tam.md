
## Technical Account Manager & AI SOC Architect

### Panther Labs (August 2025 - July 2026)

For roughly a year, I piloted Panther's first TAM program, ultimately owning over $10M USD of ARR across 17 customers.

In the summer of 2025 I was asked to become Panther's first TAM. The company had not seen this role previously, but following the work I did as the inaugural Implementation Engineer, and with the company signing larger clients, we realized the need to have a dedicated resource for these customers to support them with such a technical product and enable them to utilize it to the fullest.

Half-way through my tenure as a TAM, I was given another task: work with my clients to increase adoption of our new AI SOC functionality. We were expanding into the AI SOC space rapidly, and I worked with customers to help them get involved on the ground floor and build out what our platform could look like. Within 3 months, we successfully signed 3 AI SOC SKUs with top clients, and I developed a repeatable sales process for pitching, demoing, and closing AI SOC sales with existing customers which saw adoption from the broader sales team the following quarter.

**Responsibilities:**

- Client & Account Management
    - Act as the dedicated, primary technical resource for key clients.
    - Develop long-term relationships with business and technical stakeholders.
    - Conduct regular meetings with clients to assess product fit and strategy, address issues, and align on roadmaps.
    - Work closely with sales team to identify expansion opportunities, renewal risks, and upgrades.
- Proactive Support & Strategy
    - Run technical trainings and working sessions for new features.
    - Coordinate incident/outage response.
    - Discuss upcoming features with clients and strategize on new workflows to meet existing business needs.
- Cross-Functional Advocacy & Education
    - Advocate for customer internally by translating needs and feedback into actionable insights for product and development teams.
    - Identify strategic early adopters for features in beta and facilitate conversations between PMs and clients.
    - Communicate product releases, features, and roadmap with clients.
