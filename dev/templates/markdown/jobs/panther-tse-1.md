
## Technical Support Engineer Lv. 1

### Panther Labs (June 2022 - May 2023)

Provided frontline technical support for customers which encountered issues using Panther, or had questions about the product.

**Responsibilities**:

- Triage and respond to incoming customer tickets, and prioritize according to severity and urgency of issues.
- Reference, maintain, and contribute to internal and customer-facing informational resources, including a knowledge base, and Panther's official documentation.
- Participate in building tools and processes to improve the team's efficiency.
- Log, monitor, and help prioritize feature requests and bug reports.
- Host info-sessions to share technical product knowledge with the support team, and other less-technical teams across the org.

**Accomplishments**:

- Developed a tool to assist with automated assignments of incoming tickets. The tool tracked when users were on lunch, on break, or off-duty for the day. This ensured that tickets weren't assigned to inactive agents, which reduced mean times to first response.
- Served as a "TSE Specialist" in our engineering department, whereby I spent time shadowing engineers working in a particular domain to learn more about the product, and how to troubleshoot advanced issues.
- Regularly in top 10% of TSEs in terms of ticket resolutions per month.
