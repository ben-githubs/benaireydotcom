
## Implementation Engineer

### Panther Labs (November 2023 - July 2024)

In the months leading up to Nov 2024, myself and my grand-manager Curtis were tasked with standing up a Professional Services team at Panther. The team would provide onboarding services for new customers, and ad-hoc professional services for existing customers. Nobody had ever undertaken this task at Panther before, and we were chosen to build out this new function.

Ultimately, the team was deprecated during some restructuring, and the role was outsourced to partner teams (which I helped train up). However, during our 9-month run, we successfully onboarded 11 new customers, performed a few ad-hoc services (including data exfiltration and detection writing), and drafted a number of processes to integrate professional services into our sales cycles. After the restructure, I moved into a position as a detection engineer.

**Responsibilities:**

- New Customer Onboarding:
    - Plan, schedule, and run training sessions with customers
    - Produce training materials (documentation, presentations, videos, etc.) for use during pre- and post-sales
    - Track current engagements and report progress and metrics
    - Follow up with new customers regarding bugs, feature requests, etc.
    - Research and develop specific implementations/integrations for individual customer needs
- Ad-Hoc Services:
    - Track service requests and report progress/revenue.
    - Work with customers to understand their specific requirements and constraints.
    - Keep customer apprised of progress, blockers, and expectations.
