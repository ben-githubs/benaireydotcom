
## Detection Engineer

### Panther Labs (July 2024 - July 2025)

As a detection engineer, I got to take ownership of one of the core product experiences at Panther: managing content via CI/CD. My responsibilities were split evenly between managing the out-of-the-box detection content, and developing/maintaining the tools we provide customers for developing their own detections.

I undertook a number of projects in this role, such as:

- Migrating release and publish workflows away from `setuptools`
- Creating guardrails to more thoroughly test managed content for bugs or noise before releasing broadly to customers
- Streamlining the unit test development process by introducing a log redaction tool
- Co-developing automated red-teaming tools to provide end-to-end detection testing
