About Me

"The perfect merging of people skills and technical knowledge... but at what cost?"

Professional Stuff

I thrive when working with customers on technical problems. I have strong people skills and a solid technical foundation, coupled with a skill for teaching and breaking complex topics into digestible pieces. I've been fortunate enough to spend time in my career as a support technician, a developer, and an account rep, and those experiences allow me to connect with clients on a personal level while bringing with me a technical authority.

I enjoy helping solve difficult problems, and I approach all my customer relations as a partnership; we are working together to solve real world issues, big and small. I'm transparent when I don't know something, and forthcoming when I do. That authenticity is a huge part of what enables people to trust me in my work.
